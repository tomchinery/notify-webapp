Vision

The vision of notify is to be able to create and schedule custom notifications on every platform in one place. The idea came to me when I was frustrated with to-do app notifications, I was setting up my to-do's each day but would only get notified on one platform.

I still want to explore some features which are not in the app above. Such as being able to schedule a notification for a specific platform, better date and time selection, and using device gestures.

The reason why I say the project is out of scope of this hackathon is that I plan on pursuing this idea and releasing the app on multiple platforms in the coming months.
