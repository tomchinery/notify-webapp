How to use

  • Add notifications by clicking the plus icon.
  • Delete notifications by hovering over the left of a notification and clicking the cross icon.
  • Test notifications by hovering over the right of a notification and clicking the bell icon.
  • Edit notifications by hovering over the right of a notification and clicking the pencil icon.

Vision

The vision of notify is to be able to create and schedule custom notifications on every platform in one place. The idea came to me when I was frustrated with to-do app notifications, I was setting up my to-do's each day but would only get notified on one platform.

I still want to explore some features which are not in the app above. Such as being able to schedule a notification for a specific platform, date and time selection, and using device gestures.

The reason why I say the project is out of scope of this hackathon is that I plan on pursuing this idea and releasing the app on multiple platforms in the coming months.
